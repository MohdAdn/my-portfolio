Besan Laddu


Ingrediants

  • Sugar
  • Maida
  • Oil
  • Milk

Recipe

  1. Make a dough using Milk and Maida
  2. Deep fry in the oil
  3. Make Sugar Syrup with 4 cup sugars
  4. Put the fried Gulab Jamun in sugar syrup
  5. Garnish with Almond and serve hot
